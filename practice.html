PSYCHOLOGY

What is Psychology?

Psychology is the scientific study of the human mind and behavior. It tries to understand how people think, feel, learn, and act in different situations.



    Why is Psychology Important?

    Psychology helps us to:

  • Uerstand human behaviornd
  • Improve mental health
  • Solve problems in learning, relationships, and work
  • Understand emotions, motivation, and personality
